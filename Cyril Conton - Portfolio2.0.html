Supraconductivity

An image of quantum physics

Supraconductivity makes magnets, bullet trains levitate and MRI scans hum.

So, on the 100th anniversary of its discovery, physicist Julien Bobroff from the CNRS chose to give his schools' interventions a new visual tool, more adequate with today's theories.

The project has been used in workshops at Paris' Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie, in 2011 and is part of several exhibits, such as SUPRA! in 2018.

  • The project on «La Physique autrement» website
  • Feature in :etapes magazine n°214
  • Upcoming exposition SUPRA! in Saint-Étienne, France

The result is a leaflet, made to be downloaded and printed on any domestic printer.

In this leaflet are explained some of the basic principles of quantum physics and supraconductivity, throughout three scales: from electron to human scale.

  • Download leaflet (EN)

Each scale features a model to be made, using instructions and patterns condensed in an inner leaflet.

Minimum tools are required to realize each model: cisors, glue and/or adhesive tape.
